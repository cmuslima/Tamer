What is this study about?

This research study is being done to study the ways on how we can leverage human feedback to improve performance of autonomous systems.
You are being asked to take part because you fall in our specified age range (over 18 years old) and have the basic knowledge in operating a
computer that is necessary for the data collection. Depending on the configuration of the study you are taking part in, it can take about 15-40 minutes
for the off-line setup or 1-1 ½ hours for the on-line setup.

Are there any benefits to me If I am in this study

There is no direct benefit to you from being in this study.
However, through this study, we can understand more about the benefits of crowdsourcing or human-in-the-loop and
how it can be use to solve problems of navigating robots in the future.
There are potential benefits to educational, consumer, industrial, and military applications.
